Homestay Siti Rapeah

“Alamat”

Homestay Siti Rapeah offers a unique opportunity for visitors to Pengudang to immerse themselves in the local culture by living together with the villagers. With three well-equipped rooms, each with its own toilet, air conditioning, and other necessary amenities, guests can enjoy a comfortable stay while still being able to experience the local way of life. For an unforgettable and authentic experience in Pengudang, Homestay Siti Rapeah offers a chance to live side by side with the locals while enjoying the comforts of a well-equipped accommodation. Immerse yourself in the vibrant village life, create meaningful connections, and make lasting memories during your stay.

Warung dan Homestay "Bunda"

“Alamat”

Warung and Homestay "Bunda" offer a cozy and authentic accommodation experience in Pengudang. Located adjacent to the owner's home, these homestays provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere for visitors. The homestay consist of a two-floor small building, allowing guests to have their own private space while being in close proximity to the friendly hosts. The rooms are well-maintained and comfortable, providing a peaceful retreat for guests to relax and unwind. One of the highlights of staying at Warung and Homestay "Bunda" is the opportunity to savor delicious homemade food on demand. The hosts are known for their culinary skills and are happy to prepare meals for guests upon request. Indulge in traditional flavors and local dishes, offering a taste of authentic Indonesian cuisine. Experience the comfort of a home away from home, enjoy homemade meals, and create lasting memories in this charming village.

Transporation


How to Reach The Village

To reach Pengudang Village from Raja Haji Fisabilillah International Airport in Tanjung Pinang City, visitors have the option of renting a personal vehicle in advance. Renting a vehicle provides the convenience and flexibility to explore the beautiful scenery along the way.


As you embark on your journey, you will have the opportunity to enjoy the scenic seaside road that stretches along the coastline. The breathtaking views of the sparkling sea and the gentle breeze create a serene atmosphere, making the drive an enjoyable experience. The well-maintained road winds its way through picturesque landscapes, offering glimpses of lush greenery and traditional villages along the route.
